Write a Post

Once the new post file is created, you can edit the post_name.md file. Let’s walk through some important elements of writing a Markdown post in Hexo.

Front Matter

The Front Matter is a section at the beginning of the Markdown file, enclosed between triple dashes (---). It’s used to configure various settings for your post.

Here’s an example of common fields found in Front Matter:

  • title: The title of your post.
  • date: The date and time the post was created.
  • tags: Keywords related to the content of your post, useful for grouping similar topics.
  • categories: Larger groupings to categorize posts.
  • description: A short description of the content.
  • top_img: The URL of the banner image displayed at the top of the post.

For more detailed configuration options, refer to the official documentation.
